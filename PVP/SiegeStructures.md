# Siege Structures

Siege Structures are the primary PvP objective on HavenCraft.

They combine challenging PvE encounters with Towny territory control, creating large battles between towns over valuable strategic locations.

Unlike traditional Towny wars, towns cannot permanently destroy or absorb each other. Instead, they compete for ownership of these ancient fortresses.

---

# What is a Siege Structure?

A Siege Structure is a handcrafted landmark somewhere in the world.

Examples include:

- Ancient ruins
- Fortresses
- Keeps
- Towers
- Temples
- Strongholds

Each structure is populated with powerful custom MythicMobs and contains a hidden Capture Point.

Before another town can realistically claim the structure, they must first survive its defenders.

---

# Ownership

Each Siege Structure can only be controlled by one town at a time.

The controlling town receives:

- **$1,000 every hour**
- Recognition as the owner
- Control until another town successfully captures it

---

# Starting a Capture Battle

To attack a Siege Structure:

1. Join a town.
2. Travel to the Siege Structure.
3. Stand inside its Capture Point.
4. Use:

```
/t capture
```

If another town controls the structure, a Capture Battle begins.

If nobody owns the structure, your town can claim it immediately.

---

# During the Battle

Capture Battles last:

**10 minutes**

The attacking town must reduce the Capture Point's health from:

```
50 HP
↓
0 HP
```

before time expires.

If the timer expires first, the defending town keeps control.

---

# How Capture Progress Works

On HavenCraft, **player kills do not damage the Capture Point.**

Instead, the Capture Point changes based on which team controls the objective.

This means:

- Holding the objective wins battles.
- Simply farming kills does not.

Teams must physically occupy the Capture Point while preventing defenders from reclaiming it.

---

# Defending

Members of the defending town may quickly return to the battle using:

```
/t defender
```

This ability has a cooldown of:

**60 seconds**

---

# Rewards

The winning town immediately becomes the new owner.

Every controlled Siege Structure generates:

**$1,000 per hour**

until another town captures it.

Large empires controlling multiple Siege Structures can generate substantial passive income.

---

# Battle Rules

- Maximum battle time: **10 minutes**
- Capture Point HP: **50**
- Inventory is dropped on death.
- Friendly towns within the same nation **cannot** attack each other's Siege Structures.
- Non-participants are removed from active battles if they interfere.

---

# Strategy

### Bring Allies

A coordinated group is much stronger than a lone attacker.

### Clear the PvE Defenders

Many Siege Structures contain powerful custom monsters guarding the objective.

Ignoring them is often far more dangerous than fighting enemy players.

### Control the Objective

Standing on the Capture Point is what wins battles.

Holding the area is far more important than chasing kills.

### Prepare for Counterattacks

Even after defeating the monsters, another town may arrive before the capture is complete.

Always expect PvP.

---

# Commands

| Command | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `/t capture` | Begins attacking the Siege Structure you're standing inside. |
| `/t defender` | Teleports a defending player back to their Siege Structure during an active battle. |

---

# FAQ

## Can I attack my own nation?

No.

Towns belonging to the same nation cannot capture each other's Siege Structures.

## Do player kills damage the Capture Point?

No.

Control of the objective determines capture progress.

## Do I keep my inventory?

No.

Players drop their inventory normally when they die.

## Can I build during a battle?

No.

Siege Structures are protected landmarks and cannot be modified during Capture Battles.

## Can multiple Siege Structures be owned?

Yes.

A town may control as many Siege Structures as it can successfully defend.
