# PvP

PvP on HavenCraft is centered around exploration, territory control, and risk versus reward.

Unlike many Towny servers, your towns are **safe from permanent destruction**. Instead, towns compete over valuable **Siege Structures**, powerful landmarks scattered throughout the world that generate income and become focal points for large battles.

## Wilderness PvP

The wilderness is a dangerous place.

- PvP is enabled throughout the wilderness.
- Outside of town claims, be prepared to defend yourself.
- Valuable resources, dungeons, and Siege Structures often attract other players.

Leaving the safety of your town means accepting the risk of being attacked by other adventurers.

## Town PvP

Inside your own town, normal Towny protections apply unless you intentionally enable PvP.

Enemy players cannot simply invade your town and destroy it.

Instead, territorial conflict revolves around Siege Structures.

## Siege Structures

Siege Structures are special locations found throughout the world.

Each one is:

- A unique handcrafted landmark.
- Filled with dangerous custom monsters.
- Worth a large hourly income to the town that controls it.
- Open for other towns to challenge and capture.

Ownership changes through Capture Battles rather than permanent town conquest.

Learn more here:

→ [Siege Structures](/pvp/siegestructures)

## Death

Dying in PvP works the same as normal gameplay.

Unless otherwise stated by an event:

- You drop your inventory.
- Other players may loot your items.
- Returning prepared is highly recommended.

## Tips

- Travel in groups.
- Scout before attacking.
- Bring food, potions, and repair supplies.
- Expect PvE and PvP threats around Siege Structures.
