## Summer Event Mechanics

| Mechanic | Source | Player-Facing Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Overheated | Summer Event Controller | Builds while players are on the summer island. At full Overheated, players are slowed and take fire tick damage. |
| Heat Wave | Summer Event rotation | Makes Overheated rise faster during the active event window. |
| Cooling Items | Summer Event items | Ice Cream Cone clears Overheated; Ice Cube, Soda Pop, and Popsicle reduce it by set amounts. |
| Parrot Rescue | Parrot Jail Break | Click a lost parrot so it follows, then bring it to the Parrot Trainer for a Summer Ticket. |
| BANANARAMA EXTRAVAGANZA | Event rotation | Drops bananas and banana bundles onto sand around the island. |

## Ice Pyramid Mechanics

| Ability or Mechanic | Source | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Frostbite | Ice Pyramid mobs and gear | Slows or freezes targets through frost-themed attacks. |
| Frost Nova | Frostfang and Vaelthyr | Area burst that pressures nearby players. |
| Blizzard | Glaciara | Local storm pressure around the boss. |
| Ice Prison | Glaciara | Traps a target in temporary packed ice while applying damage pressure. |
| Frost Slash | Frostforged Blade | Right-click active that sends out a frost cone. |
| Frost Ward | Eternal Frost Amulet | Right-click defensive effect that helps prevent frostbite buildup for a short window. |
