# Dungeons

HavenCraft is a **dungeon progression server**. Dungeons are not side content — they are the main path forward. Each dungeon you clear unlocks the next stage of the game: new gear, new materials, new enchants, new features.

## How Progression Works

- **Dungeons release in order and scale in order.** Dungeon 1 assumes you own nothing; Dungeon 2 assumes you cleared Dungeon 1.
- **Loot from one dungeon carries you into the next.** The Viking Stronghold hands out starter armor, weapons, and Tier-I enchant tomes; Castle Nocturn expects that gear and rewards you with diamond-tier loot and Tier-II tomes.
- **Souls are the through-line.** Every dungeon mob drops [soul currency](/items/souls) — fragments from trash mobs, guaranteed souls from bosses.
- **Bosses gate the goods.** Signature relics, crown shards, and keys only come from boss kills.
- **Queue with a group before entering.** Bring food, repair items, and the right resistance gear. Bosses have phase mechanics — read room warnings. Loot is usually awarded after the final boss dies, and leaving early can end your active run.
