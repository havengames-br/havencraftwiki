# Dungeons

HavenCraft is a **dungeon progression server**. Dungeons are not optional side content — they are the main path forward. Each dungeon you clear unlocks the next stage of the game: new items, new gear, new crafting recipes, new currencies, and new features.

## How Dungeon Progression Works

- **Dungeons are gated and ordered.** You progress through them in sequence, starting with Dungeon 1.
- **Clearing a dungeon unlocks the next.** Beating a dungeon's final boss opens the path to the dungeon that follows it.
- **Each dungeon unlocks new content.** Dungeons are the source of their own themed items, materials, gear, recipes, and gameplay features.
- **Gear up before you climb.** Loot and crafted upgrades from earlier dungeons are what let you survive the later ones.

Work through the progression in order. If you skip ahead, you will be under-geared for the content that comes next.
