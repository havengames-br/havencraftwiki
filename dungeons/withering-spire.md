# Withering Spire

> **Dungeon 3** · In Development · Diamond-to-Netherite difficulty

The Withering Spire is HavenCraft's third dungeon — a rot-choked tower and its surrounding swamp, built for players who've cleared [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) and want the next real check on their gear. Its own drop-table notes describe it as "medium-difficulty... for players in full diamond to full netherite," a clear step up from the castle's vampires.

Everything here leans on poison: withering bogs and gas vents soak the floor, a witch coven curses from the corners of its own boss room, and a rot matriarch commands the whole tower. Four bosses stand between you and the Spire's treasure.

**A note on status:** this dungeon hasn't shipped yet. Its internal display name is still the unconfigured default, it has no assigned release date, and several pieces of designed gear and loot tables aren't wired up yet — see the [loot page](/items/withering-spire) and individual monster pages for the specifics. "Withering Spire" is the working title used throughout its files and drop tables.

## Mechanics & Points of Interest

- **Poison is the whole dungeon.** Nearly every monster and hazard here inflicts Poison or Confusion rather than raw damage. Bring cleanses — Milk Buckets work in a pinch, and Castle Nocturn's [Jar of Holy Water](/items/potions) clears Poison too.
- **The Corrosion Ritual.** Partway through her fight, [Verdelith](/monsters/withering-spire/verdelith) roots in place and summons four killable Toxin Anchors that heal her while they live. Kill them fast — the ritual's climax nuke lands regardless, but every anchor you drop is healing she doesn't get.
- **The coven corners.** The [Hag Matriarch](/monsters/withering-spire/hag-matriarch) teleports to the corners of her own room and channels her sisters' signature attacks against the party before blinking back. Four [Suspicious Cauldrons](/monsters/withering-spire/suspicious-cauldron) also cycle between healing and harmful auras around her arena — use the calm phase, don't get caught in the green one.
- **Grothmire never stops calling reinforcements.** The Spire's ooze titan summons colored Slime Minions on nearly every attack — let them pile up and a long fight gets much harder than it needs to be.
- **Souls.** This dungeon's currency is the [Toxic Soul](/items/souls), dropped by Aldric/Bilepod, Verdelith, and (rarely) other Spire monsters.

## Loot Overview

Full item details live on the [Withering Spire loot page](/items/withering-spire).

- **Treasure tiers:** diamonds, quartz, obsidian, slime balls, gold ingots, and netherite scrap, scaling across five named tiers from common trash up through a dedicated (if not yet fully wired) Cuboss tier.
- **Toxic consumables:** Venomtip Arrows, Toxic Splash Flasks, and Spider Eyes drop constantly; Draughts of Haste and Enchanted Golden Apples are rarer finds, best odds from bosses.
- **Beheading III books:** a 10% drop from all four bosses.
- **Designed but not yet dropping:** a full bespoke gear line — Toxic Fang, Toxic Bow, Toxic Shovel, Toxic Shield, Repulsor's Grasp, and the four-piece Matriarch armor set — exists in the game's files but isn't wired into any drop table yet.
- **Souls:** [Toxic Soul and the shared soul system](/items/souls).
