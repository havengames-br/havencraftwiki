# Items

## Selling Items

Most items in the game — ores, crops, mob drops, gear, blocks, and more — can be sold to the server for money with `/sell hand`, `/sell all`, or `/sell <item> <amount>`. See the full **[Sell Prices](/sell)** list for what everything is worth.

## Sockets, Gems & Familiar Stones

New cross-dungeon systems introduced alongside the loot rebalance that pulled starter gear off trash mobs:

- **Socketed Iron Gear** — armor pieces and swords with an empty gem socket, dropped in place of plain starter gear.
- **Gems** (e.g. Sapphire Guard, Moonstone Balance, Garnet Might) — slot into socketed gear for bonus effects.
- **Common/Greater Familiar Stone** — bind to summon a companion. Seen dropping in both the Viking Stronghold and Castle Nocturn.

Full drop rates currently live on the [Viking Stronghold loot page](/items/viking-stronghold#sockets-gems--familiar-stones) — this section will likely grow its own page as more dungeons pick it up.
