# Castle Nocturn Loot

Custom items from [Dungeon 2](/dungeons/castle-nocturn). Castle Nocturn's loot is a full step above the Viking Stronghold: diamonds and emeralds off trash mobs, rare netherite, Tier-II tomes, and the Sanguine crafting line.

## Nocturn Tomes

Tier-II enchant books. ~5–6% from regular mobs, guaranteed from bosses (plus a 50% second roll).

| Tome | Enchant |
| --- | --- |
| Nocturn Tome — Protection II | Protection II |
| Nocturn Tome — Sharpness II | Sharpness II |
| Nocturn Tome — Power II | Power II |
| Nocturn Tome — Efficiency II | Efficiency II |
| Nocturn Tome — Unbreaking II | Unbreaking II |
| Nocturn Tome — Feather Falling II | Feather Falling II |
| Nocturn Tome — Looting I | Looting I |

**Beheading I books** also drop here — [Blood Acolytes](/monsters/castle-nocturn/blood-acolyte) are by far the best source at 20%, and bosses have a 50% chance.

## Treasure

Trash mobs drop straight treasure: diamonds and emeralds at 4–22% depending on the mob, and netherite ingots from 0.1% (trash) up to 12% (bosses). Bosses guarantee 8–14 diamonds and 8–14 emeralds per kill.
