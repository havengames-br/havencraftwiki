## Notes

- **Blank Keys are boss loot.** Every dungeon boss has a strong chance to drop one, so a full clear usually pays out several.
- **A blank key wants cutting.** Its lore says it all — hold onto them until you find who (or what) cuts keys.
- **Viking Keys are the rarest drop in Dungeon 1.** Any Viking Stronghold mob can drop one, but at 1-in-1000 you'll likely see a Frozen Crown before you see one of these.
- **The Locksmith cuts Blank Keys into themed keys.** A Blank Key plus the matching dungeon's soul — Frozen Soul or Sanguine Soul — gets you a Frozen or Blood Key. Frozen Key is no longer an unpredictable "special reward"; it's a standing Locksmith trade (see [Traders](/traders)).
