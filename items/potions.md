# Potions & Elixirs

Custom consumables beyond vanilla brewing. Dungeon-dropped drinks are also listed on their dungeon's loot page.

## Combat Brews

| | Potion | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <img src="/assets/items/potions/potion_of_momentum.png" alt="" width="32"> | **Potion of Momentum** | +2% damage per consecutive hit, stacking up to 10 times. Stacks expire if you don't land a hit within 2.5s. Lasts 30s. |
| <img src="/assets/items/potions/potion_of_greater_momentum.png" alt="" width="32"> | **Potion of Greater Momentum** | +3% damage per consecutive hit, up to 10 stacks, same 2.5s rule. Lasts 45s. |

Momentum brewing starts with [Momentum Powder](/items/viking-stronghold#consumables--materials) from Living Cinders.
