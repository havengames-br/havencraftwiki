# Souls & Currency

Souls are HavenCraft's global dungeon currency. Every custom dungeon mob drops them on a shared tier system — weak mobs shed fragments occasionally, elites guarantee them, and bosses drop true souls.

## Drop Tiers

Every custom mob carries one of these soul tiers:

| Tier | Mobs | Drop |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Summoned adds and weak mobs | 1 Soul Fragment, 20% chance |
| 2 | Normal dungeon mobs | 2–5 Soul Fragments, 35% chance |
| 3 | Elites and mini-bosses | Soul Fragments, guaranteed |
| 4/5 | Dungeon bosses | 1 Warrior Soul guaranteed, plus a 50% chance at the dungeon's themed soul (ex. Frozen or Sanguine) |



## What Are They For?

Souls feed dungeon crafting and upgrades. Hold onto everything — themed souls in particular are boss-limited, so they're the slowest to farm. Watch the [Items](/items) pages: recipes that consume souls are documented with the gear they create.
