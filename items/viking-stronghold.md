## Frostbound Tomes

Tier-I enchant books. Now roughly 1.5–6% from casters and elites (Viking Shaman, Raven Sorceress, Viking Sentinel, Living Cinder) — regular Viking Warriors, Drunken Raiders, and Frost Wolves no longer carry them. Guaranteed from the Raider Quartermaster, Cindius, and the High Priestess (on the Vessel's death); Asvald does not drop one.

## Runebound Tomes

A rarer, higher tier above the Frostbound Tomes — real chase-loot enchant books carrying custom Emberstrike, Frostbite, or Stormstrike enchantments (each with I/II/III power levels). Combine with a weapon on an anvil.

## Standard Gear

Starter equipment no longer drops "constantly from trash mobs" — as of the loot rebalance, only dungeon chests and boss kills reliably hand out leather/chainmail/iron armor pieces and starter weapons. Regular trash and elite mobs (Viking Warrior, Drunken Raider, Viking Shaman, Raven Sorceress, Viking Sentinel, Living Cinder, Frost Wolf) now pay out in currency and materials instead: iron, gold, diamonds, lapis, Small Repair Kits, Gems, Socketed Iron Gear, and Familiar Stones. See each [monster page](/monsters) for exact rates.

## Sockets, Gems & Familiar Stones

New loot categories introduced in the same rebalance that removed trash-mob gear drops:

- **Socketed Iron Gear** — plain iron armor pieces and swords with an empty gem socket. Drops from several trash and elite mobs, and guaranteed from Asvald (50%).
- **Gems** (e.g. Sapphire Guard, Moonstone Balance, Garnet Might) — slot into socketed gear. Low-chance drops from elite mobs.
- **Common/Greater Familiar Stone** — bind to a summoning crystal to call a companion. Drop from elite mobs, chests, and bosses.

These appear to be shared systems used across more than one dungeon — check [Items](/items) or [Enchants](/enchants) for a fuller writeup if one exists.
