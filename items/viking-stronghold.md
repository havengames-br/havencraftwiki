# Viking Stronghold Loot

Custom items from [Dungeon 1](/dungeons/viking-stronghold). The stronghold is the starter dungeon, so most of its relics are marked *starter relic — cannot be mended or enchanted*: they're meant to carry you to Castle Nocturn, not to endgame.
