| --- | --- | --- |
| Frostbitten Thrall | Husk | Slow melee bruiser that applies frost pressure. |
| Glacial Stalker | Stray | Ranged frost archer. |
| Rimeclaw Lurker | Cave Spider | Fast pouncing crawler. |

| Permafrost Brute | Zombie | Elite heavy enemy with ground slam pressure. |
