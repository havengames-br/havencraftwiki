# Ashen Revenant

*"You will not pass."*

An axe-bearing wall of ash and hate stationed at the castle's choke points. The Revenant exists to stop your push, and it has the toolkit for the job: slams, roots, and a temper that gets worse as it gets hurt.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — gates and chokepoints

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 40 |
| Damage | 6 |
| Threat | High |

## Abilities

- **Shockwave Slam** — periodically slams the ground, damaging and scattering everyone around it.
- **Grave Grasp** — roots the nearest player in place. Being held next to a 6-damage axe is how people die here.
- **Enrage** — below 35% health it can fly into a rage. Finish it fast once it's low.
- Heavily knockback-resistant.
