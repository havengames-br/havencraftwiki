# Blood Acolyte

*"The master's hunger is my hunger."*

Robed hexers who serve the Veil from the backline. An acolyte left alone will bolt you from range, ward itself, drain your life to feed the ritual — and if you finally corner one, it detonates the blood around it.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — often clustered around [Ritual Anchors](/monsters/castle-nocturn/ritual-anchor)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 30 (ritual-summoned: 20) |
| Damage | 1 (ritual-summoned: 4) |
| Threat | Medium — priority target |

## Abilities

- **Blood Bolt** — its bread-and-butter ranged hex, fired every few seconds.
- **Acolyte's Ward** — periodically shields itself.
- **Drain Ritual** — channels a life-drain on the nearest player.
- **Crimson Nova** — get within 4 blocks and it erupts in a blood nova. Burst it down or fight it at range.
- **Retreat** — constantly repositions away from melee.

During ritual events, killing the summoned acolytes is what damages the [Ritual Anchor](/monsters/castle-nocturn/ritual-anchor) (or the [Crimson Blood Anchor](/monsters/castle-nocturn/ritual-anchor#crimson-blood-anchor) in Vraelith's throne room).
