# Crimson Thrall

*"...blood... give me blood..."*

The castle's most common horror — mortals drained and remade as servants of the Veil. Every wound they deal feeds them, so a thrall you ignore is a thrall that heals.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — the most numerous enemy in the castle

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 50 |
| Damage | 5 |
| Threat | Low alone; dangerous in packs |

## Abilities

- **Lifesteal** — every hit on you heals the thrall.
- **Blood Latch** — a small chance (8%) on hit to latch onto you and hold you in place.
- **Frenzied Lunge** — below 40% health it starts lunging desperately at you.
