# Cursed Skeleton

Black-boned soldiers of the castle, each one carrying a different weapon loadout — and a nasty surprise. When a Cursed Skeleton falls, don't sheathe your sword: its first death is a lie.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 50 |
| Damage | 5 |
| Threat | Medium |

## Abilities

- **Curse on Hit** — 25% of its attacks afflict you with a curse. Carry [Holy Water](/items/potions) or wear the [Cleansing](/enchants#sentinels-ward--defensive-armor) enchant.
- **False Death** — collapses on "death," but that's only the real end about half the time. The rest of the time it either detonates for extra damage or gets back up at low health with its original weapon. Don't sheathe your sword early.
- **Random Loadout** — spawns with varied weapons, so no two packs fight quite the same.
