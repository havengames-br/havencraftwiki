# Cursed Statue

An armored figure on a pedestal. It was in a different spot a second ago. You're sure of it.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | Unkillable — immune to every damage type |
| Damage | **100** — lethal through any armor |
| Threat | Lethal hazard |

## Behavior

- **It only moves when nobody is looking at it.** Line of sight freezes it in place; turn your back and it comes for you.
- **One touch kills.** Its strike deals 100 damage — no gear survives that.
- **It cannot be destroyed.** No damage type affects it. Fire, magic, arrows, explosions — nothing.

## How to Survive

- In a group, assign a spotter: one player keeps eyes on the statue while the others move.
- Solo, back away facing it. Never turn and run.
- It only strikes at extremely close range — distance is safety.

## Drops

Nothing. There is nothing to kill.
