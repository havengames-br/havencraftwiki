# Dark Rider

A wither-black lancer on a skeletal horse, riding endless patrol routes through the castle grounds in a trail of ash and shadow. Spot the black particles in the distance and you have a choice: ambush, or be ambushed.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — patrolling the grounds

| Stat | Dark Rider | Dark Patrol Steed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Health | 120 | 80 |
| Damage | 8 (iron spear) | 0 |
| Threat | High | Low |

## Abilities

- **Patrol** — rides fixed routes until it acquires a target.
- **Combat Charge** — spurs the steed into a charge once it spots you.
- **Dismount Fighting** — the rider can drop from the saddle mid-combat and keep fighting on foot. Killing the horse first grounds him permanently.
- Both rider and steed are knockback-resistant and track targets from very far away.
