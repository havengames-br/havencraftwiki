# Guardian Golem

An iron golem standing sentry in the castle's halls — but this one was built by someone with a sense of humor and a supply of explosives. Guardian Golems are rigged: get close and the trap springs.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — guarding rooms and corridors

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 120 |
| Damage | 12 |
| Threat | Trap / heavy melee |

## Abilities

- **Detonation Trap** — primed to detonate when triggered. Approach like it's going to explode, because it is.
- **Golem Fists** — if the blast doesn't get you, 12-damage punches will.
- Locks onto any player within 15 blocks.
