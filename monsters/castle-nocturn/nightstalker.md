# Nightstalker

*"Heh heh heh..."*

You'll hear it before you see it — and you'll stop seeing it right when you think you've won. The Nightstalker is the castle's assassin: fast, vicious, and gone the moment the fight turns against it.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 50 |
| Damage | 12 |
| Threat | Medium-high — highest trash damage in the castle alongside the Revenant |

## Abilities

- **Shadow Dart** — flings shadow bolts at you from close range.
- **Blood Latch** — 10% chance on hit to latch on and hold you.
- **Shadow Vanish** — below 40% health it melts into shadow to escape and reset. Kill it before it slips away or you'll fight it again at full aggression.
