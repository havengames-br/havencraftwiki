# Oathbreaker

> **Boss** · Colossus

The camera pans. The ground shakes. Something that was never meant to wake is waking anyway. The Oathbreaker is a towering armored colossus bound beneath the castle — a boss fight introduced by its own cinematic, and a wall of stone and spectral steel once it starts swinging.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 800 |
| Damage | Heavy, ability-driven |
| Resistances | Takes only 50% of projectile damage — **fight it in melee** |
| Other | Immune to knockback; becomes damage-resistant while stunned |

## Abilities

- **Awakening** — the fight opens with the colossus rising. Use the time to position.
- **Triple Swings** — cycles through massive sweeping combos in melee range.
- **Swarm Missiles** — launches homing projectiles at range, constantly.
- **Ghost Swords** — spectral blades erupt from the ground where players stand, striking the area around them. Watch for the summoning flash and step out.
- **Strafing footwork** — for its size, it repositions constantly. Don't trust its slowness.
