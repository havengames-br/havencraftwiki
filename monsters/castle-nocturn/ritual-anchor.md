# Ritual Anchor

A floating, rune-scarred altar at the heart of a blood ritual circle. The anchor itself cannot be scratched — its life is bound to the acolytes who feed it.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — ritual chambers

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 100 (damaged only by acolyte deaths) |
| Damage | 0 |
| Threat | Objective mechanic |

## How It Works

- **Immune to all damage.** Swords, arrows, fire, explosions — the anchor ignores everything.
- **Spawns waves.** While it stands, it periodically summons defenders into the chamber.
- **Killed through its acolytes.** Each summoned [Blood Acolyte](/monsters/castle-nocturn/blood-acolyte) you kill near the circle wounds the anchor. Kill enough and *"the ritual circle shatters as the last acolyte falls."*

## Strategy

Ignore the anchor entirely — it's a health bar you can't touch. Focus every kill on the acolytes and mind their close-range Crimson Novas while you do.

## Drops

None — breaking the ritual clears the way forward.
