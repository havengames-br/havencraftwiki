# Vampire Laborer

The castle still runs on labor, and the labor never gets to rest. These pickaxe-swinging vampires work the castle's blood-veined seams — which makes them the only miners of **Sanguine Ore** in the game.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — the work tunnels

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 35 |
| Damage | 10 |
| Threat | Medium — hits far harder than its health suggests |

## Abilities

- **Mine Drain** — 25% of its pickaxe hits drain you as if you were ore.
- Fast mover; closes distance quicker than most castle mobs.
