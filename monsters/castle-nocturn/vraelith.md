# Vraelith, Lord of the Crimson Veil

> **Final Boss** of Castle Nocturn

*"Even the night... grows cold."*

The vampire lord himself — one of the largest health pools on the server and a fight that changes shape repeatedly on the way down. Vraelith taunts, teleports through mist, calls the swarm, unleashes a whole arsenal of bat-themed attacks, and ends the fight under a blood moon.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — the throne of the Veil

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 1,000 |
| Damage | 8 (plus heavy magic) |
| Boss bar | Red — *Vraelith, Lord of the Crimson Veil* |
| Resistances | **Immune to fire.** Takes only 60% of projectile damage. Immune to knockback. |

**Leave the Flame bow at home.** Fire does literally nothing to him, and arrows are weak — this is a melee slugfest.

> If a [Crimson Blood Anchor](/monsters/castle-nocturn/ritual-anchor#crimson-blood-anchor) is still standing in his throne room when the fight begins, Vraelith spawns with a large bonus to his health pool. Break the anchor first.

## The Fight

Vraelith layers abilities in bands as his health falls:

- **Mist Travel** (most of the fight) — dissolves into mist and reappears on the nearest player.
- **Veil Magic** (90%–20%, on and off through most of the fight) — barrages of crimson bolts, short and long range.
- **Shadow Phase** (85%–20%) — phases through shadow to reposition and strike.
- **Red Winds** — a scouring wind-and-blood barrage that hits at three health checkpoints: roughly 70%, 50%, and 5%.
- **Bat Swarm** (60%–30%) — floods the room with lesser [Blood Bats](/monsters/castle-nocturn/blood-bat) (5 HP, 1 damage) and drain beams.
- **Firebat Volley** (90%–60%) — fires a trio of dragon-fire projectiles.
- **Bat Barrage** (80%–50%) — a homing bolt of bat-fire that bursts on impact.
- **Blood Ring** (80%–40%) — conjures a spinning ring of fire around himself.
- **Wing Slam** (around 60% and again around 10%) — launches airborne, turns damage-resistant, and rains bat-fire down before landing.
- **Bat Ultimate** (around 40%) — fully transforms into a bat and takes to the air for about 35 seconds.
- **Crimson Wrath** (40%–10%) — his blade work turns frenzied.
- Between 41%–5% health he also lashes out with one of four desperation attacks — a rage strike, dragon flame, a meteor barrage, or a fireball volley.
- **Blood Moon Eclipse** (30% and below) — the endgame. Survive it.

Every swing of his blade also carries randomized sweeping slash attacks, and he shrugs off all knockback — you cannot peel him off a teammate.
