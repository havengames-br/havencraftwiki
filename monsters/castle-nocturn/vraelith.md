# Vraelith, Lord of the Crimson Veil

> **Final Boss** of Castle Nocturn

*"Even the night... grows cold."*

The vampire lord himself — the largest health pool on the server and a fight that changes shape five times on the way down. Vraelith taunts, teleports through mist, calls the swarm, and ends the fight under a blood moon.

**Found in:** [Castle Nocturn](/dungeons/castle-nocturn) — the throne of the Veil

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 1,250 |
| Damage | 8 (plus heavy magic) |
| Boss bar | Red — *Vraelith, Lord of the Crimson Veil* |
| Resistances | **Immune to fire.** Takes only 60% of projectile damage. Immune to knockback. |

**Leave the Flame bow at home.** Fire does literally nothing to him, and arrows are weak — this is a melee slugfest.

## The Fight

Vraelith layers abilities in bands as his health falls:

- **Mist Travel** (most of the fight) — dissolves into mist and reappears on the nearest player.
- **Veil Magic** (early–mid) — barrages of crimson bolts, short and long range.
- **Shadow Phase** (85%–20%) — phases through shadow to reposition and strike.
- **Red Winds** (80%–40%) — scouring winds sweep the arena.
- **Bat Swarm** (60%–30%) — floods the room with lesser [Blood Bats](/monsters/castle-nocturn/blood-bat) (10 HP, 6 damage).
- **Crimson Wrath** (below 40%) — his blade work turns frenzied.
- **Blood Moon Eclipse** (below 30%) — the endgame. Survive it.

Every swing of his blade also carries randomized sweeping slash attacks, and he shrugs off all knockback — you cannot peel him off a teammate.
