# Asvald, The High Viking

> **Final Boss** of the Viking Stronghold

Lord of the frozen sanctum and the strongest fighter in the stronghold by far. Asvald is an axe duel that keeps escalating — six phases, each unlocking more of his arsenal, with the sky darkening overhead for the whole fight.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold) — the Frozen Sanctum

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 750 |
| Damage | 3 (melee) + heavy ability damage |
| Armor | 3 |
| Boss bar | Blue — *Asvald, The High Viking* (darkens the sky) |
| Resistances | Takes only a third of projectile damage — **this is a melee fight** |

## Abilities

- **Aqua Slash** — his signature strike, thrown both on attack and on a timer.
- **Water Wave** — sends a wave surging across the arena.
- **Disaster** — periodically unleashes havoc directly in front of him. Don't stand in his face when he winds up.
- **Warden Shadows** — invisible, smoke-wreathed shades (15 HP, 5 damage) join the fight to pressure your backline.
- **Six phases** — at 84%, 68%, 52%, 36%, and 20% health he transitions, layering on new ability patterns each time. The last stretch is the deadliest.

Asvald is the only source of Frozen Crown Shards — the blacksmith might know what to do with them, and the Dungeonmaster is said to want the finished [Frozen Crown](/items/viking-stronghold#boss-uniques).

**Asvald's Axe** is his own signature weapon — a rare (5%) drop mirroring the fight itself: Aqua Slash (8 damage) on left-click, an Aqua Smash ice barrage (up to 21 damage, 15s cooldown) on right-click, a Water Wave (8 damage, launches foes, 5s cooldown) on shift-right-click, and a Frozen Sword Rain (25 damage, 15s cooldown) on shift-left-click.
