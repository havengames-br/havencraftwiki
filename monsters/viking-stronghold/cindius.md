# Cindius, Flame of Valhalla

> **Boss** · Fire

The great flame that the [Living Cinders](/monsters/viking-stronghold/living-cinder) splintered from. Cindius doesn't chase you around the arena — it hangs in place, a wreath of fire around a dark core, and turns the room itself against you.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 250 |
| Damage | 0 (ability-based) |
| Boss bar | Red — *Cindius, Flame of Valhalla* |

## Abilities

- **Sky Bomb** — its favorite move: fiery bombardment called down from above. Keep moving.
- **Infernal Pull** — occasionally drags players toward its burning core instead.
- **Drain Bookcases** — feeds on the room's shelves to sustain itself.
- Immobile but immune to knockback; picks targets across the whole room.
