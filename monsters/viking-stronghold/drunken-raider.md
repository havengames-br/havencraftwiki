# Drunken Raider

Three times the health of a Viking Warrior and more than twice the punch — the ale hasn't slowed his axes any. He stumbles, he drinks mid-fight, and he hits like a longboat.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 30 |
| Damage | 5 |
| Threat | Medium |

## Abilities

- **Stumble** — lurches unpredictably as he fights. Harder to kite than he looks.
- **Drink Ale** — periodically swigs from his supply mid-combat.
- **Double Swing** — 18% of his attacks come as a rapid double hit.
