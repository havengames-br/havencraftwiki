# Frost Orb

A hovering sphere of frost wreathed in snowflakes. Frost Orbs are wards, not fighters — they hang in place, rain frost strikes on the room, and chill everyone near them. And your sword is useless against them.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold) — and empowered versions guard the [High Priestess of Odin](/monsters/viking-stronghold/high-priestess-of-odin)'s rituals

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 80 |
| Damage | 0 (ability-based) |
| Threat | Mechanic — area denial |

## Abilities

- **Frost Strike Rain** — periodically calls frost strikes down around itself.
- **Chill Aura** — pulses slowness onto everyone nearby.

## How to Destroy It

The orb is **immune to melee and magic**. It only takes damage from:

- **Fire** — fire damage of any kind, including fire spells and standing lava
- **Burning fire arrows** — normal arrows are cancelled mid-flight unless the orb is already burning, so **Flame-enchanted bows are the tool of choice**

Bring a Flame bow (or fire charges) into the stronghold and orbs become a formality. Without one, they're a locked door.

## Drops

Nothing — destroying the ward *is* the reward.
