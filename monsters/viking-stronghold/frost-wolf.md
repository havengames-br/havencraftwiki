# Frost Wolf

Fast, white-furred pack hunters that run with the viking raiders. Individually fragile, but they rarely come alone — Viking Warriors can call them mid-fight, and the **Frost Wolf Howler** variant leads stronger packs.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold)

| Stat | Frost Wolf | Frost Wolf Howler |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Health | 5 | 10 |
| Damage | 2 | 4 |
| Speed | Fast | Fast |

## Behavior

- Sprints straight at you and bites. No tricks — just teeth and numbers.
- Howlers aggressively target players on sight; regular wolves join whoever's fighting.
