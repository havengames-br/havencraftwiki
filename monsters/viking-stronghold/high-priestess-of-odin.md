# High Priestess of Odin

> **Boss** · Two-stage encounter

She isn't what she first appears. What looks like an inert idol in her sanctum — the **High Priestess Vessel** — is only a shell. Crack it, and it dissolves into golden dust and a portal to a higher realm, where the true **High Priestess Divine** is waiting. Bring her low too, and she refuses to die: she ascends, heals to full, and keeps fighting.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold) — the sanctum, and beyond it a portal to a higher realm

| Stat | Vessel | Divine |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Health | 400 | 700 |
| Damage | 1 (melee) + ability damage | 1 (melee) + ability damage |
| Armor | 2 | — |
| Boss bar | Yellow — *High Priestess Vessel* | Pink — *High Priestess Divine* |

## Abilities

**The Vessel (400 HP).** She stands motionless and unarmed until you land the first hit — then she arms herself and the fight begins for real.

- **Radiant Beam, Gravity Pull, Starfall Nova, Sacred Charge** — a rotating set of light counter-attacks whenever you're in melee range of her.
- **Light Pulse** and **Gentle Volley** — periodic AoE pulses and homing light-bolts aimed at anyone nearby.
- Around a third health she gets a one-time "I will not fall so easily" heal; below a quarter health she goes invulnerable and plays out a scripted death — she isn't actually dead yet.

**The Divine (700 HP).** Killing the Vessel opens a portal. Step through, and the true High Priestess is waiting — and she never loses aggro once she's locked on.

- Same melee-counter kit as the Vessel, plus a rotating set of larger casts: **Light Rain**, **Divine Passage** (blinks around the arena firing homing bolts), **Storm Rage**, **Skyfall Volley**, **Radiant Beams**, **Ground Flash**, and **Celestial Tempest** — each telegraphed with dialogue and a beacon-charge windup.
- **Won't stay down.** Brought below roughly 200 of her 700 HP, she refuses to die: fully heals, ascends into an unrelenting "Goddess Mode" — permanently airborne, raining projectile volleys and meteors — and periodically tops herself back off while above ~200 HP again.
- In Goddess Mode she adds three heavier finishers: **Judgment**, **Flash of Terror**, and **Divine Lights**.
- Only falls for good once brought back under roughly 100 of her 700 HP a second time.

This is a long fight with a false death built into the middle of it — don't relax when the Vessel goes down.
