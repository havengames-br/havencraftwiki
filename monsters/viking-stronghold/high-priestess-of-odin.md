# High Priestess of Odin

> **Boss** · Ritualist

Mistress of the Order of Odin and keeper of its rites. She is a fight about interruption: let her rituals complete and the room fills with frost and the faithful; break them and she stands exposed.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold) — the ritual chamber

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 300 |
| Damage | 4 |
| Armor | 2 |
| Boss bar | Yellow — *High Priestess of Odin* |

## Abilities

- **Awakening of the Faithful** — when struck, she rouses Fallen Viking Spirits: soul-wreathed ghosts that drift toward her and empower her when absorbed.
- **Odin's Call** — periodically calls lightning down on a random member of your party.
- **The Great Rituals** — at **75% and 30% health** she begins a ritual and becomes **invincible while it channels**, summoning empowered [Frost Orbs](/monsters/viking-stronghold/frost-orb). Destroy the orbs to end the ritual — remember, only fire and burning arrows hurt them.

**Bring a Flame bow.** The ritual phases are a hard wall without fire damage.
