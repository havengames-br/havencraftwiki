# Living Cinder

An arcane flame given hunger. You won't see a body — just a drifting knot of fire particles feeding on the stronghold's bookcases. It announces itself with its own red boss bar when you get close.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold) — the library

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 100 |
| Damage | 0 (ability-based) |
| Threat | Medium — mini-add |

## Abilities

- **Drain Bookcases** — feeds on nearby bookshelves to sustain itself.
- **Random Fire Pulse** — periodically erupts in a burst of flame at whoever's near.
- **Bodiless** — invisible and immune to knockback; track it by its fire particles.
