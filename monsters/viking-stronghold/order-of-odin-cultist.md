# Order of Odin Cultist

A robed cleric standing quietly in the stronghold's halls. He won't attack. He won't even move. He's just... watching.

Strike him, and the disguise drops.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 20 |
| Damage | 0 (until transformed) |
| Threat | Trap |

## Behavior

- **Transforms when damaged.** Attacking a cultist triggers his true form — expect a real fight where a villager stood.
- Until then he is completely passive, standing still and tracking you with his eyes.

## Drops

The cultist himself drops nothing — the fight (and the loot) comes from what he turns into.
