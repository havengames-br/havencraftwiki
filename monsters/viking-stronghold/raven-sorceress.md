# Raven Sorceress

A purple-robed witch with a raven-skull staff, holding the stronghold's darker corners. She fights from range, curses the whole party, and calls down her flock — and when she dies, she doesn't go quietly.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 40 |
| Damage | 7 |
| Armor | 2 |
| Threat | Medium — elite |

## Abilities

- **Call Ravens** — periodically summons divebombing ravens that harass everyone nearby.
- **Feather Hex** — a recurring curse cast across all players in the fight.
- **Death Rite** — triggers a final effect on death. Don't stand in whatever she leaves behind.
- Keeps 18+ blocks of distance and backs away from melee.
