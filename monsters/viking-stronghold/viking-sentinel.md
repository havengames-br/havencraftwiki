# Viking Sentinel

A wall of iron and spear planted in the middle of a hallway. The Sentinel doesn't chase — he doesn't need to. He holds his ground, shield raised, and punishes everything that steps into reach.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold) — guarding a key hallway

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 140 |
| Damage | 7 |
| Armor | 6 |
| Threat | Elite |

## Abilities

- **Hold the Line** — immovable: completely immune to knockback and slow to reposition. This fight happens on his ground.
- **Spear Wall** — constantly strikes everything in range with his iron spear.
