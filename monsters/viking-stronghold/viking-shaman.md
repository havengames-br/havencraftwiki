# Viking Shaman

A staff-wielding mystic who hangs back from the melee, keeping his warband alive and hurling wolf hexes at anyone who pushes in. He deals no melee damage himself — his staff does the talking from range.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 15 |
| Damage | 0 (caster) |
| Threat | Low, but a priority target |

## Abilities

- **Wolf Hex** — fires a cursed wolf projectile at his target every few seconds.
- **Healing Ritual** — periodically channels a ritual that heals nearby vikings. Kill the shaman first or you'll fight the same warriors twice.
- **Keeps his distance** — actively backs away to stay ~18+ blocks from you.
