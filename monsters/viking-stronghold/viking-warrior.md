# Viking Warrior

The backbone of the stronghold's garrison. A dual-axe raider in chainmail and leather who closes fast and swings faster.

**Found in:** [Viking Stronghold](/dungeons/viking-stronghold)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 10 |
| Damage | 2 |
| Armor | 6 |
| Threat | Low |

## Abilities

- **Axe Throw** — periodically checks range and hurls an axe at distant targets. Don't assume you're safe at bow range.
- **Call the Pack** — a small chance (1%) when damaged to summon Frost Wolves to his side.
