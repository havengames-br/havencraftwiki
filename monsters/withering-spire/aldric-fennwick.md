# Aldric Fennwick, the Rotten Vessel

> **Boss** · Two-stage encounter

*"Ah... Verdelith sent a guest. No... wait. You killed my acolytes. I can taste their absence on you. Brave. Stupid. But brave."*

What greets you first is Aldric — a bloated, bottle-hoarding golem of a man who fights with grabs, thrown tonics, and summoned metal wards. Bring him down and he doesn't die: he chugs one last tonic from his satchel and his body splits open. **Bilepod, the Swollen Host** claws its way out and the real fight begins.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire)

## Aldric Fennwick (750 HP)

- **Melee Combo** — a rotating mix of two-hit strike combos and a **Grab**: get caught within 4 blocks and he mounts you on his back for two hits before dismounting you into a disorienting concussion field.
- **Potion Throw** — bottles up and lobs a toxic tonic that bursts into a damaging poison cloud.
- **Summon Circle** — when nobody is in melee range, calls a ring of animated metal wards around himself; each one winds up and fires a tracking spike at a target before crumbling.
- **Stun Phases** — at roughly 50% and 25% health he braces, goes briefly invulnerable, and calls in a ring of metal wards while stunned. *"You... you cannot fathom what I have already survived."* / *"The vessel cracks! No matter — the work continues!"*
- **Not a real death.** Drop him to 0 and he doesn't fall — he drinks from his satchel and Bilepod bursts free.

## Bilepod, the Swollen Host (500 HP)

Slow, tanky, and falling apart at the seams. Bilepod trades Aldric's ranged tricks for pure attrition: a poison proc on every hit, a room-wide burst if you ignore the telegraph, and a corpse that isn't done fighting even after it dies.

- **Melee Proc** — roughly half of Bilepod's hits poison you on top of the damage.
- **Lurch** — a short charge at its target that drags a trail of acid pool hazard behind it.
- **Gorge** — the headline mechanic. Its gut swells over three escalating warnings (~5.5 seconds total) before a guaranteed room-wide burst: 9 damage up close, a wider poison cloud, and two [Volatile Sludge](/monsters/withering-spire/volatile-sludge) summoned. There's no way to stop it — only to clear out before *"It's about to burst!"*
- **Rupture** (once, ~45–50% health) — a permanent speed buff, a poison pulse, and one more Volatile Sludge summoned.
- **Frenzy** (once, ~15–20% health) — refreshes the speed buff to a higher level, adds a permanent damage buff, a bigger poison pulse, and two more Volatile Sludge.
- **Death is a corpse-bomb.** After it finally dies, the body hisses for about 3 seconds before detonating for 12 damage and a wide poison cloud. Don't rush the loot.
