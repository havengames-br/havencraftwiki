# Cursed Spore Blossom

A rooted, glowing growth that looks like part of the scenery until you wander too close. Half ambush plant, half ranged shooter — it punishes both getting near and staying at range.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire) — a common sight through the dungeon's overgrown rooms

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 40 |
| Damage | 0 (ability-based) |
| Threat | Low |

## Abilities

- **Idle Pollen** — a faint glow while at rest, giving away its location if you look closely.
- **Trigger Burst** — gets within 4 blocks of it and it bursts repeatedly over several seconds, choking the air with poison and confusion.
- **Bolt Shoot** — while in combat, fires green poison bolts at range (6 damage + poison), so backing off doesn't make it safe.
