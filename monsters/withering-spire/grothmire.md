# Grothmire, the Weeping Ooze

> **Boss** · The Ooze Titan

A hulking, faceless mass of living slime — invisible until it commits to the fight, then a wall of stomps, backhands, and gatling-fire slime shot that never stops calling in more of itself. Grothmire is the dungeon's single toughest health pool and, by its loot table's own billing, the intended capstone fight of the Spire.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire)

## Abilities

Grothmire is stationary and invisible until a player triggers it, then locks on and cycles through a weighted set of attacks — mostly **Slime Hand**, filled out with stomps, spitfire, and repositioning:

- **Slime Hand** (most common) — rears back and slams a wide forward line for 10 damage; telegraphed with a warning rectangle.
- **Stomp** — slams the ground in a 9-block radius for 12 damage, telegraphed with a warning circle under a random target.
- **Spitfire** — a gatling burst of slime rounds, 3 damage per hit, fired in a long stream at its target.
- **Dissolve** — melts into the floor, goes briefly invincible, then reappears near a player.
- **Move Closer** — lunges toward its target if nobody stays within range.
- **Slime Minions** — Stomp, Slime Hand, and Spitfire all have a chance to summon a **Slime Minion** (Green, Blue, Red, or Yellow) alongside the attack. Minions are weak on their own (10–40 HP, 2–4 damage) but Grothmire can flood the room with them over a long fight — clear them so they don't stack up.
