# Hag Matriarch

> **Boss** · The Coven Mother

*"Another meal wanders into my kitchen... let's see how long you last before you rot."*

The witch coven's leader and easily the most mobile of the Spire's bosses — she teleports constantly, channels her sisters' signature attacks from the corners of her own room, and calls the coven in when she's hurting. Four [Suspicious Cauldrons](/monsters/withering-spire/suspicious-cauldron) stand around her arena, cycling between healing mist and toxic bursts.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 1,000 |
| Damage | 8 |
| Boss bar | Blue — *Hag Matriarch — The Coven Mother* |
| Resistances | Immune to knockback; takes only 20% of projectile damage |

## Abilities

**Passive (all phases)** — a constant poison aura and a rotating attack of **Poison Volley** (a triple poison-burst thrown at range) and **Toxic Cleave** (a melee poison nova), plus **Bubbling Ground** — a delayed totem that erupts under a player's feet for 6 damage and poison.

**Phase 1 (100%–70%)** — the base rotation above, cycling every few seconds.

**Phase 2 (70%–35%)** — she starts teleporting to the corners of the room and channeling her coven-sisters' signature attacks against the whole party before blinking back to center and hitting the nearest targeted spot for bonus damage:

- **Corner: Apothecary** — channels a rapid triple poison barrage, themed after the [Toxic Apothecary](/monsters/withering-spire/toxic-apothecary).
- **Corner: Repulsor** — pulls the party in, then blasts everyone within 8 blocks for 6 damage, a big knockback, and Wither — themed after the [Vile Repulsor](/monsters/withering-spire/vile-repulsor).
- **Corner: Voidbinder** — drops multiple void zones that detonate for 12 damage and poison.
- **Corner: Cauldron** — fills the room with a toxic gas pulse for several seconds, themed after the boss room's own [Suspicious Cauldrons](/monsters/withering-spire/suspicious-cauldron).
- **Sisters! To me!** (once, ~50%) — summons one [Toxic Apothecary](/monsters/withering-spire/toxic-apothecary) and one [Vile Repulsor](/monsters/withering-spire/vile-repulsor) as reinforcements.
- Occasionally summons a pair of [Toxic Frogs](/monsters/withering-spire/toxic-frog) as well.

**Transition — "ENOUGH! I'll rot you where you stand!" (~20%, once)** — she stops teleporting away and commits to the fight.

**Phase 3 (below 35%, and a fog pulse from 70%–20%)**

- The full corner rotation continues, now including her own Repulsor-burst and Voidbinder-field attacks directly.
- **Phase 3 Fog** — a wide, room-filling poison cloud (30-block radius).
- **"FROM THE ROOTS TO THE RAFTERS — DROWN IN IT!"** (once, ~10%) — a telegraphed room-wide ultimate: 10 damage and heavy poison out to 25 blocks.

Rotted Hags occasionally throw up a decoy **witch clone** (a 10 HP illusion that giggles and lobs a potion before vanishing) — don't waste a real combo on one.
