# Noxious Bloom

Disguised as an inert slime block, hiding in plain sight among the dungeon's slime terrain. It never attacks directly — it doesn't have to. Left alone, it just keeps spitting sludge at you until you're forced to close the distance and kill it.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire) — a rare, stationary elite (only a few placed in the whole dungeon)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 60 |
| Damage | 0 (ability-based) |
| Threat | Elite |

## Abilities

- **Idle Glow** — a faint pulsing glow that's the only real tell it isn't scenery.
- **Summon Sludge** (every 12s) — spits out a [Volatile Sludge](/monsters/withering-spire/volatile-sludge) toward nearby players.
- **Death Burst** — ruptures into a wide, lingering cloud of toxic spores on death, pulsing poison in bursts across several seconds.
