# Rotted Hag

The most dangerous of the coven's rank and file, built around void magic rather than straight poison. She's the [Hag Matriarch](/monsters/withering-spire/hag-matriarch)'s rank-and-file counterpart — same coven, same rough silhouette, meaner toolkit. Full drop table and kit are wired up, but no confirmed spawn point turned up in the current dungeon data — see the note on the [dungeon page](/dungeons/withering-spire).

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire) — witch coven

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 50 |
| Damage | 4 |
| Threat | Elite |
| Resistances | Takes only 20% of projectile damage |

## Abilities

- **Toxic Potion Throw** — the coven's shared poison-bottle throw, 8 damage plus poison and slow.
- **Voidbinder Field** (every ~5s once triggered) — drops a telegraphed void zone that detonates for 10 damage and confusion.
- **Push** — knocks the whole party back hard with Wither, then conjures a trio of **witch clones**: 10 HP decoys that giggle, lob a potion, and vanish. Don't waste a real combo on one.
- **Juicystaff** — charges her staff, then unleashes a triple-tick void field for 4, 10, and 6 damage in succession.
