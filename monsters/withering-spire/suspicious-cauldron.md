# Suspicious Cauldron

Four bubbling cauldrons sit around the [Hag Matriarch](/monsters/withering-spire/hag-matriarch)'s arena. Unlike the Spire's other hazards, these aren't purely dangerous — they cycle between a calm, helpful phase and a harmful one, and reading the cycle is part of surviving the room.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire) — four, all in the Hag Matriarch's boss room

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | Unkillable — invincible, no AI |
| Damage | 0 directly (see cycle below) |
| Threat | Objective mechanic |

## How It Works

Each cauldron runs on a constant cycle:

- **Good Aura (pink, ~16s)** — a soothing pink mist. Standing near it clears your Poison and Slowness and grants Regeneration.
- **Warning (~1.5s)** — the mist fades and a bell starts ringing rapidly. *"...the cauldron fades. GET CLEAR!"*
- **Bad Aura (green, ~5s)** — the cauldron erupts: a guaranteed poison burst, plus a 50% chance to also trigger a small toxic fissure eruption. Both hit anyone standing close.
- The cycle repeats for as long as the cauldron exists.

## Strategy

Use the good phase to top off and cleanse between Hag Matriarch's attacks, but don't linger once the bell starts ringing — get clear before the aura flips green.

## Drops

None — it's an unkillable environmental fixture, not a monster to fight.
