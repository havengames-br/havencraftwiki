# Toxic Apothecary

The coven's brewer — a bow-armed witch who keeps her distance and drenches you in poison from range. In the current build she isn't scattered through the dungeon like her sisters; the only confirmed way to meet one is as a reinforcement the [Hag Matriarch](/monsters/withering-spire/hag-matriarch) calls in partway through her fight.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire) — summoned by the Hag Matriarch at roughly 50% health

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 60 |
| Damage | 4 |
| Threat | Elite |
| Resistances | Heavily resistant to ranged damage (source data lists a truncated projectile-resistance value — see the dungeon page notes) |

## Abilities

- **Toxic Potion Throw** — lobs a poison bottle that bursts for 8 damage, poison, and slow on impact.
- Standard bow attacks at range between throws.
