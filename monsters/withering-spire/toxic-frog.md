# Toxic Frog

A bloated, poison-slicked frog that closes distance in single leaping bounds. Individually harmless, but it's everywhere — the Spire's most common standalone spawn — and the [Hag Matriarch](/monsters/withering-spire/hag-matriarch) calls in pairs of them as reinforcements too.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire) — throughout the dungeon, and summoned by the Hag Matriarch

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 20 |
| Damage | 4 |
| Threat | Low |

## Abilities

- **Leap** — bounds toward its target every couple of seconds; hard to outrun in a straight line.
- **Poison on Hit** — every melee hit poisons you.
- **Poison Pulse** — periodically poisons anyone standing within 2.5 blocks, leap or no leap.

## Drops

No dedicated drop table is currently wired to this mob — it doesn't appear in `WitheringSpireDrops.yml`. Treat it as a free kill for experience until that's confirmed otherwise.
