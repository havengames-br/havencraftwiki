# Verdelith, the Rot Matriarch

> **Boss** · The Rot Matriarch

*"The air thickens with the stench of decay."*

The tallest health pool in the Spire and the dungeon's signature set-piece fight. Verdelith's arrival is a full cutscene: eight beams of light converge around the arena before she tears free of the ground with a warden's roar. From there it's three escalating phases, a scripted ritual intermission, and a desperation nuke on the way down.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire)

## The Fight

**Phase 1 — Spore Awakening (100%–70%)**

- **Melee Proc** — most of her hits carry a poison tick.
- **Toxic Bolt Barrage** (every 8s) — a volley of three rotting-spore bolts, 4 damage + poison each.
- **Pollen Nova** (every 14s) — her flesh blooms with spore-sacs before erupting in an expanding poison-and-confusion pulse.

**Transition — "Enough games." (~70%, once)**

**Phase 2 — Sludge Surge (70%–40%)**

- **Acid Slam** (every 16s) — slams the ground for 6 damage in a 3.5-block radius and tears open a Miasma Geyser that lingers nearby.
- **Sludge Bolt Fan** (every 12s) — fires the same bolt volley at everyone within 25 blocks.
- **Toxic Blight** (~70% and ~50%, twice total) — she melts into a writhing ooze, turns damage-resistant and rooted, then unleashes rings of tracking toxic spikes (6 damage per hit) around herself before leaping back into the fight.
- **Sludge Sphere** (~60% and ~40%, twice total) — takes to the air on levitation, then rains a spread of poison bolts (4 damage each) down around the arena for several seconds.

**Corrosion Ritual (~55%, once)**

*"Kneel. Let the rot take root!"* Verdelith roots in place and summons four **[Toxin Anchors](#toxin-anchor)** around the arena, and calls in three [Cursed Spore Blossoms](/monsters/withering-spire/cursed-spore-blossom) at the edges. She channels for roughly 12 seconds; when it ends she unloads a guaranteed nuke — 10 damage to anyone within 8 blocks, poison out to 20 blocks — regardless of how many anchors are still standing.

**Transition — "THE ROT CONSUMES ME!" (~40%, once)** — a permanent speed buff kicks in.

**Phase 3 — Rot Frenzy (below 40%)**

- **Withering Collapse** (every 26s) — gathers a withering cloud for 3 seconds, then bursts for poison, confusion, and 4 damage in a 6-block radius.
- **Bog Slam** (every 18s) — erupts a [Withering Bog](/monsters/withering-spire/toxic-hazards#withering-bog) (Large) and two Smalls around the arena.
- **Enrage Aura Pulse** (every 10s) — a short-range poison pulse.

**Last Gasp (~20%, once)** — *"IF I ROT, YOU ROT WITH ME!"* A telegraphed nuke (poison + 6 damage in a 6-block radius), four [Volatile Sludge](/monsters/withering-spire/volatile-sludge) summoned, and a plague-spread barrage that rains bolts across the whole room.

### Toxin Anchor

Four stationary adds summoned during the Corrosion Ritual — unlike Castle Nocturn's Ritual Anchors, these aren't immune to damage; they're a real, killable priority target.

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 50 |
| Damage | 0 (poison pulse only) |
| Threat | Ritual add |

- Every ~3 seconds, pulses poison on anyone within 3 blocks **and heals Verdelith for 25 HP** — every anchor left standing is free healing she didn't earn.
- Killable, and worth killing fast: the ritual's climax nuke lands no matter what, but fewer anchors means less healing banked and less poison stacked on the party beforehand.
