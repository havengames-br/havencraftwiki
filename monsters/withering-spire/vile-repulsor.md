# Vile Repulsor

The most common member of the witch coven by far, and not confined to their lair — you'll run into these scattered through most of the dungeon. A ranged bow-caster whose signature trick is knocking you clean off your feet.

**Found in:** [Withering Spire](/dungeons/withering-spire)

| Stat | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Health | 50 |
| Damage | 4 |
| Threat | Elite |
| Resistances | Takes only 20% of projectile damage |

## Abilities

- **Toxic Potion Throw** — lobs a poison bottle that bursts for 8 damage, poison, and slow on impact.
- **Repulsor Burst** (every ~4s once triggered) — a telegraphed cast that hits everyone within 7 blocks for 10 damage, launches them hard, and inflicts Wither. This is what earns the name — expect to get shoved out of the fight.
- Standard bow attacks at range in between casts.
