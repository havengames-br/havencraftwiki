# Traders & NPCs

Not everything on HavenCraft wants to kill you. These are the NPCs and services worth knowing.

## Hub NPCs

| NPC | Where | What They Do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Nanny Ethel** | Spawn hub | Welcomes new arrivals near the spawn teleporter. |
| **Garbage Bin** | Spawn | A cauldron-shaped bin — interacting with it destroys the item in your hand. Careful what you're holding. |

## Event Traders

| NPC | Where | What They Do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Parrot Trainer** | Summer Event island | Rescue Lost Summer Parrots and bring them close to her — each returned parrot pays out a **Summer Ticket**. See [Events](/events). |

## Dungeon Figures

Some "traders" would rather rob you than trade:

- The [Raider Quartermaster](/monsters/viking-stronghold/raider-quartermaster) hoards the Viking Stronghold's supplies. He doesn't sell — but killing him spills the whole stockroom: ingots, arrows, food, and gear.
- The **blacksmith** is said to know what to do with [Frozen Crown Shards](/items/viking-stronghold#boss-uniques), and the **Dungeonmaster** wants the completed Frozen Crown. Ask around at spawn.

## Selling Loot

Vanilla materials from dungeon runs (metals, gems, food, mob drops) can be sold to the server economy. Prices favor honest grinding — mass-farmable items are cheap, and charcoal is famously the intended money grind. Check current prices in-game before hauling.
