# Traders & NPCs

The HavenCraft hub is staffed by NPC traders clustered near spawn. Almost everything they sell is bought with [souls](/items/souls) — Soul Fragments, Lost Souls, Warrior Souls, and the dungeon-specific Frozen/Sanguine Souls — so dungeon runs double as shopping trips. None of these NPCs go by a proper name in-game; they're listed here by what they do.

## The Dungeonmaster

The rank-up NPC. He wants proof you've beaten each dungeon's final boss, and rank grants real progression: new dungeons and Towny access.

| Give | Get |
| --- | --- |
| [Frozen Crown](/items/viking-stronghold#boss-uniques) | **Squire Rank** — unlocks additional dungeons |
| [Vraelith, Lord of the Crimson Veil](/monsters/castle-nocturn/vraelith)'s head | **Knight Rank** — unlocks additional dungeons and claiming in the Towny world |

This is the payoff for the [New Player Path](/home): clear the Viking Stronghold, craft the Frozen Crown from your [Frozen Crown Shards](/items/viking-stronghold#boss-uniques), and turn it in here for Squire. Clear Castle Nocturn and bring back Vraelith's head for Knight.

## The End Merchant

An enderman, not a citizen — and the only trader not selling dungeon loot. Deals exclusively in Lost Souls and Ender Pearls for End-themed gear and building blocks, including seven numbered "Ender Dragon Ball" trophies. What they're for isn't clear yet — keep an eye on future content.

| Give | Get |
| --- | --- |
| 13 Lost Souls + 5 Ender Pearls | Ender Dragon Ball (1 of 7) |
| 1 Lost Soul | 5 Ender Pearls |
| 1 Lost Soul | 64 End Stone |
| 1 Lost Soul | 64 End Stone Bricks |
| 1 Lost Soul | 64 Purpur Block |
| 1 Lost Soul | 16 Purpur Stairs |
| 1 Lost Soul | 64 Purpur Pillar |
| 10 Lost Souls + 16 Ender Pearls | Elytra |
| 1 Blank Key + 16 Ender Pearls | Ender Key (crate key) |
